About

👶 Family

My greatest joy is being a mom. Watching my son discover the world every day is endlessly entertaining — and exhausting in the best possible way. I'm also mom to two Alaskan Klee Kai mini husky dogs, who keep the household lively and full of personality.

⛰️ Travel

I have a deep love for travel and believe the best way to understand the world is to experience it firsthand. From exploring local food markets to navigating new cities on foot, every trip leaves me with a fresh perspective and a longer bucket list.

💻 Career

I work in private equity, where I focus on evaluating investments and driving value creation across portfolio companies. I enjoy the analytical rigor the field demands and the opportunity to work alongside talented teams building something meaningful.

🏃 Running

Running marathons has become one of my biggest passions and a way I challenge myself outside of work. My PR journey spans three World Majors — New York City (3:50), Chicago (3:43), and Berlin (3:33), where I set my personal best.
